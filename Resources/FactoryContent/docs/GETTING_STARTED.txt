LumenDSP — Getting started
==========================

1. Open LumenDSP (standalone) or load the VST3 in your DAW.
2. Standalone only: click Audio and choose your guitar input and speaker/headphone output.
3. Play — factory preset "01 Glass Clean" loads automatically on first launch.
4. Browse presets for Airy Clean, Smooth Lead, Full Rig Clean, Light Crunch, and Ambient Swell.
   Each factory tone uses the full path: gate, comp, drive, EQ, cab, delay, and reverb.
5. Optional: import your own .nam models and .wav cab IRs from Library, then Save a preset.

See QUICKSTART.md in the project repository for the full guide.
